    Jake Rich submitted a new resource:

    Upgrade Workbenches - Lets you upgrade workbenches.

    Allows players to upgrade workbenches!

    To upgrade, drag a higher tiered workbench into the inventory of your current workbench.

    When you upgrade, you will receive back your old workbench in item form.

  11. k1lly0u

    The error has nothing to do with plugins or Oxide.

    An Imposter (otherwise known as a billboard) is essentially a image of a object used in LOD systems. In this case the imposter's are most likely trees, when you get X distance away from a tree model the model is replaced with a 2D sprite to create a better looking scene while dramatically reducing the amount of polygons the game has to render.

    All these trees you see in the distance, and probably even those few just down the hill and on the shoreline, are actually just an image of the model

  2. Wulf Community Admin

    There's no way to restart your server with just a plugin, all they can do is shut your server down. Same as with Rust's "shutdown" and "restart" commands, those are just shutting down your server. The only way to automatically restart is if your host has that ability.
     
    Wulf, Sep 21, 2015

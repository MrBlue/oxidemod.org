  3. mvrb

    MERX said: ↑
    How do I do ? What is the file or edit the command to do? my nickname at stake is "Mike" if it can help.
    Click to expand...
    The first time you use the plugin you need to save a master inventory.

    To do this, spawn the items you want to save in your inventory (armor, belt and main inventory) then type:

    /admin master

    After that, you and the other admin(s) will be able to use

    /admin

    The players with permission can then save their own private loadout by using

    /admin save

    They can then (if they have permission) change their preferred loadout to either master or their own by using:

    /admin set master
    or
    /admin set custom


    Note: You will have to save the master inventory after a restart, until I have fixed the problem with the plugin.

  2. sqroot

    I am pretty sure this issue can be caused by teleportation.
    The DeathNotes plugin suffered from the following issue:
    Player A starts a teleport. Player A fires a bullet. Player B is hit and goes down. Player A is teleported. Player B dies. The distance difference is calculated and way too large.

    However, Rust doesn't detect "invalid projectiles" on death, but on hit. That means that the player would need to teleport right between firing the bullet and it hitting its target.
    Certainly possible, but unlikely without being intended.

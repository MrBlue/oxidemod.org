    GuildManager - Gain the ability to modify guilds.

    The plugin makes it possible to modify guilds.
    Ever had a guild that had an improper name? Now you can change it for them.

    Commands:
    List:
    /listguilds - Gives a list with all guilds on the server.
    /getguild (guildname) - Gives the guild info of a guild.
    /getguildplayer (playername) - Gives the guild info of a player.
    Modify:
    /addtoguild (playername) (guildname) - Adds a player to a guild.
    /changeguildname (guildname) (newname) -...

  2. Findles

    I believe you can do this with just a server command
    try
    server.planttick x
    - Plants tick every x seconds. This is how many seconds between ticks. 60 default
    server.planttickscale x
    - Setting this to 2 will make plants grow, fruit and die two times faster than normal. 1 default.

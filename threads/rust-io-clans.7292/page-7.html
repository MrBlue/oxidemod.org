  8. dcode

    .fexXx[o_O] said: ↑
    I didnt. I only have your plugin istalled. Im talking about FF in YOUR plugin.
    Click to expand...
    FriendlyFire is also my plugin.

    Believe me or not, but this is how this works:

    The Clans plugin does not manage friendly fire on its own. What it actually does is, in case Rust:IO is installed, add clan mates to each others friendslist so that they are able to see each other on the map.

    The FriendlyFire plugin, on the other hand, disables friendly fire for Rust:IO friends. Hence: You absolutely must have this installed if there is any form of friendly fire mechanic that apparently originates from the Clans plugin.

    The solution to your issue therefore is to either delete the FriendlyFire plugin in case that you do not want friendly fire to be disabled for Rust:IO friends, or edit either of the plugins to make custom modifications. Update: There is now also a configuration options for that.

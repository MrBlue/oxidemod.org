  3. maxaki

    The best way I've come across to find an aimbotter is to 1: Get a crosshair plugin 2: Record while spectating.

    When recording and the person is in a firefight you're able to see the action multiple times in slowmotion. An aimbot is a software that will do similar patterns while shooting. Also, for convenience it's nice to turn on the record and leave the computer(Go on another PC, ofcourse) and let it record for couple hours then come back and review the footage.

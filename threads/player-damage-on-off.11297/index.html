    This is a request for a simple mod to turn on and off KoS (Kill on sight)/player damage at will.

    This is sorely needed, and I think I speak for a lot of admins. There are only two other mods that feature a similar function.

    And the other mod which features KoS on/off has temporarily disabled that function, but the mod seems to be abandoned as it hasn't been updated in at least two months.

    KoS is one of the biggest sources of complaints in-game on a lot of servers, so I hope someone who has experience with code could write a simple mod like this, it would be fantastic and much appreciated!

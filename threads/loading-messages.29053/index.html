  1. VVoid

    VVoid submitted a new resource:

    Loading Messages - Show your own customizable texts on loading screen.

    This plugin allows you to display custom texts on loading screen.
    It replaces two status strings at a fixed rate with your desired content.



    Highlights:
    • Unity's Rich Text feature is supported.
    • Can specify many messages and cycle over them.
    • Does not affect players in Queue.
    • Can specify the "last message" which will be shown when player is receiving data.

  2. Deicide666ra

    I don't know if you can change the temperatures but you can certainly change the effect it has on players. Heat doesn't seem to do much other than adding a visual effect but still the BasePlayer class contains a field that tells you the temperature where the player is located (I don't know if there's hook you can use to detect it, but you can certainly use a timer and check for it every X seconds and apply damage or effect then if the player has a certain heat level). Cold is even easier because it will trigger a hook when the damage is applied. You could easily apply a multiplier to this damage and increase/decrease the damage depending on latitude.

    I have plans for a metabolism mod that would increase the effects of cold/heat and the speed at which food/water is consumed depending on it and other conditions. Haven't got around to it yet.

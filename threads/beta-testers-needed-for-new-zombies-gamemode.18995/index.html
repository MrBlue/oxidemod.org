    Hi guys,

    We are looking for as many beta testers as possible to aid us in getting our game mode ready for release. Trials so far have proven extremely fun. Developers PaiN and LaserHydra have worked tirelessly and the game is now 95% complete. We are now just looking to balance variables and improve current features.

    Game Insipiration:
    The game is similar to CoD: MW3 Infected. Inspiration was also taken from a vCoD Zombie Mod I used to play back in 2007.

    Gameplay:
    • Games last around 10 minutes each.
    • A group of survivors must attempt to survive against a player-controlled zombie (zombie = fully naked character with a bone club).
    • The zombie must try and infect all the survivors before they are rescued at the 'Extraction Point'. The zombie has enhanced health and increased damage, they must melee a survivor with a bone club to 'infect' them.
    • If a survivor is infected, they become a zombie and must try to kill the remaining survivors.
    • For survivors to win the round, they must get to and hold the 'Extraction Point' til a helicopter arrives to pick them up in time.

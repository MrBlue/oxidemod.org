    Pilatus9 submitted a new resource:

    NoName - Plugin automatically kick players with less than 4 characters in the name

    The plugin automatically kick players with less than 4 characters in the name.

    You can withlist somebody, just give him the "noname.allowed" permission

    For it use:
    Code:
    {% raw %}
    grant user <name> noname.allowed
revoke  user <name> noname.allowed
grant group <name> noname.allowed
    {% endraw %}

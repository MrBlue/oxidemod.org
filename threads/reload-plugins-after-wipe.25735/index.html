    I am not sure how your control panel looks like in Fragnet, but this procedure should apply there as well.I assume you have a switch there with two options; no mod and Oxide ? If so, select no mod from your list then choose update/verify.After that is done, select Oxide and then choose update/verify again.When that is done, all your plugins should automatically load back and work.

    Of course the only exception would be a plugin that may be outdated or dysfunctional with newest update, but that rarely happens.

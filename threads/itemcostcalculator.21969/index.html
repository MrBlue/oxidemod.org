  1. Absolut

    Absolut submitted a new resource:

    ItemCostCalculator - Automatically Calculates Item Costs based on ingredients

    This plugin serves one specific purpose and this purpose can save someone a ton of time.

    As a server owner that once used GUIShop and ServerRewards I always struggled with pricing items. It was hard to determine what should cost what and furthermore if it was fair. In comes this plugin.

    This plugin inspects each item in the game, breaks down the components needed to make it, and prices the item based on the values set for components in the config file.

    and the pack of weapons or rockets cost 1000
    then I will give 75% of the cost in 10 hours as reward
    then 750 /10 = 75 per hour
    75% will be in "easy mode"

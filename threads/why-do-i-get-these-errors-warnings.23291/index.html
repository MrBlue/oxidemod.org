  2. Wulf Community Admin

    1. The global namespace pollution warnings (not errors) are coming from Airdrop Extended, which isn't really maintained and very outdated.
    2. CupboardForFriends is outdated or needs a fix, report to the author in the plugin's support thread.
    3. RandomSpawner outdated or needs a fix, report to the author in the plugin's support thread.
    4. Your Clans configuration may be invalid, but report the error to the author in the plugin's support thread.
    5. Your MagicLoot configuration file is invalid/corrupt.

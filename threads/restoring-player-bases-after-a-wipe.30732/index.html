  6. Ventrex

    Just like @sativaflash94 said I should use copypaste if I was you.
    Play a little bit with the radius like "/Copy World r 1000 s true". Of give your friend permission to copy and paste themself.
    Also note the size and seed of your map, if you use the same size and seed you can use: "/pasteback World a true" and everything will be back at the location you copied them.
    If you had to wipe the server you have to kill yourself so respawn at your sleeping bag.

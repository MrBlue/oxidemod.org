  1. Wulf Community Admin

    It's me again!

    We've decided to go ahead and open registration at the new site (umod.org) earlier than originally planned. The core functionality and majority of features are mostly implemented and available for use, but we will be continuing development and improving on it as we move forward. All downloads and various pages here will automatically redirect to their new counterparts at umod.org.

    If you see any plugins missing, don't worry! We're still in the process of migrating plugins over. This is a time consuming process and won't happen overnight, so bear with us and try to resist the urge to spam the forums about it.

    A recap on previous announcements here: uMod - Welcome to umod.org! - Announcements - Community

    We'll see you over at umod.org!

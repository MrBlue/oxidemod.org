  2. Steenamaroo

    I apply a fractional damage multiplier to their damage-taken to get the same effect.
    The downside is that health isn't accurately report on servers that have a visual readout.

    In OnEntityTakeDamage     I have "info.damageTypes.ScaleAll(multiplier);"

    If multiplier is 0.5f, effective health is 300.

  8. DogManJr

    Because the server is not sending that text to your computer. That text is hardcoded in the game. However, text has many ways to be represented in a computer. When your game looks up what text to display, it finds a bunch of 1's and 0's. The method your computer uses to interpret this sequence is what determines if you see "Correct Text" or "֐֐߻߻߻߻߻"

    Another reason is that your game files became corrupt. And so the location where your game looks to find that text is not even the place that has the text.

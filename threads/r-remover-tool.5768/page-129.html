    Thats not a bug, thats simple game logic. When a player enters or leave the range of a TC, the player is triggered either to get influnced by the TC or to leave the influence. If the TC is simple removed, there's no game logic which (un)triggers again to remove the influence, and that leaves him with buildung blocked, since he's technically still in the trigger...In deep its also a bit more complicated^^

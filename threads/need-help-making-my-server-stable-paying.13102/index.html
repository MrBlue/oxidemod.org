    None of those are errors, only style warnings. They will not affect the plugins or game at all. The compiler logs are generally for plugin developers, not end-users. Everything you need is usually found in the oxide_ prefixed log files.

    The time warning messages just tell you that something took longer than expected. Most of the time they are not a concern unless they are frequent, which would usually indicate your server or hardware is performing poorly.

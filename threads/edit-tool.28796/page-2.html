    Yes, it's possible. There are some model objects that do not have a visible structure. For example, the most popular place now is the city of Bandits. If you scan the chairs that stand in the playing hall where the big wheel is, you will see that there are visible chairs models "chair.static" and invisible models "chair.invisible". When copying the visible chairs, everything will be as usual. But the sofas themselves are just a picture. They can not be copied or moved. And inside of them are ordinary invisible chairs. When copying sofas, you copy only the invisible chair. )) And when you insert, you will not see anything at the insertion point. Only the name of the object.
    It will be difficult to get rid of invisible walls. If you know exactly where these walls are, then you can see the name using the tool, although nothing will appear before you. I do not know which walls you copied. Even the invisible walls may not interfere, but what was copied from the environment. Perhaps this is an object, maybe even water, only it is the back of the structure to you and it is invisible, but the sound you hear.
    In order to scale out the space from all strange objects, it is necessary to build up this place with the structures of buildings, a large foundation and there are many many walls on it. You can build two or better three floors in this way. And then using the command "/ remove all" from another plugin, delete everything. Then, together with the construction, invisible objects are removed.

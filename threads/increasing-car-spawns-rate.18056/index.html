  2. Noviets

    Download: SpawnConfig for Hurtworld | Oxide
    Then in the generated config file, look for "RoachSpawner": {
    It should be at the very top from what I remember.
    Then change "SecondsPerSpawn" to whatever you want.
    Save the file, then type "spawn.reload" in the console. Or in-game hit F1 and type "rcon spawn.reload"

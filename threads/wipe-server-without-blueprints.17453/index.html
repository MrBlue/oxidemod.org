  2. TheDoc

    There are two ways:

    #1 - change the seed, and you get a nice new map but retain the BPs

    #2 - delete your saved map files and it will wipe it. (look for this folder : C:\<Server Home Dir>\server\<server.identity>\save\Procedural Map_9999_9999999999) this will also retain your saved BPs.

    The BPs are stored in this file: UserPersistence.db - don't touch that and you will be good

    That's it.

Discussion in 'Plugin Requests' started by OfficialJaco, May 16, 2018.

  1. OfficialJaco

    I really like the game mode "Push" from the Battlefield games, and I was wondering if any plugin developers would enjoy the challenge of re-creating a private plugin of it for me.

    The goal would be to have a rectangle zone broken into multiple sections. When you enter the game you can choose to play on Team 1, or Team 2. There would be two locked crates on the defenders side (with adjusted times for unlocking it) and the goal would be for the attackers to hack the crates to continue the push. The attackers would have a time limit to do so, and if they are successful, the defenders must fall back to the new location to defend, with two more locked crates the attackers would then have to push.

    I also wanted there to be classes for different weapon choices; Assault (LR, AK, Semi-Rifle, Attachments, Metal Gear), Grenedier (Pump-Shotgun, Rocket Launcher, 3 HV rockets, 5 Grenades, Metal Gear), Support (M249, M92, Spaz shotgun, Extra Ammo, Roadsign Gear) and Sniper (Bolty, 4x scope, Coffee can helmet, no body armor). Something along the lines of that.

    Once a player dies, his body wont be lootable to avoid players getting attachments from other classes and what not, and would have a respawn timer of 10-20 seconds.

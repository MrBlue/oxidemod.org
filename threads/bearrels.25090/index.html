    ichaleynbin submitted a new resource:

    Bearrels - Random chance of bears spawning when a barrel breaks.

    This plugin sets a random chance of a bear spawning at the position of a destroyed barrel. All barrels types are included, and the percent chance must be an integer (5 not 5.0).

    To change the chance of a bearrel occuring, an admin (auth level 2) can use the chat command /bearrels [int]. Integers higher than 100 or lower than 0 will be capped at 0 or 100.

    If you want a bear to spawn with every single barrel destruction, use "/bearrels 100."

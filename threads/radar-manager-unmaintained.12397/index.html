    Clan Radar - Allows clan members and mutual friends to see others locations.

    Allow your clan members (and friends) to see where each other are on the map within a radius (or the whole map).

    Notes
    • "StartHour" and "EndHour" allows you to define between what in-game hours radar can be used. Players with active radar at EndHour will have their radar disabled. Administrators are immune to hour restrictions.
    • "Radius" defines the range in which radar will detect clan members or friends.
    • Custom permissions allow you to define different radius sizes...

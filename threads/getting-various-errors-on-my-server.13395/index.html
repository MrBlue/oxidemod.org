    The auth ticket messages and lack of identity is generally a non-Steam client trying to connect to your server.

    The RPC error is from a plugin trying to do something to a player that doesn't exist anymore.

    The ignored sockets errors I am unsure about.

    The rest of the messages can be ignored.

  3. Grokeezy

    And I ended up figuring it out immediately after posting, sorry for post but for anyone interested in the future, this is all you need to do:
    Code:
    {% raw %}
                EmoteManagerServer emTest = session.WorldPlayerEntity.GetComponent<EmoteManagerServer>();
            emTest.BeginEmoteServer(EEmoteType.Surrender);
    {% endraw %}

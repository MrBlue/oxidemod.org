    sami37 submitted a new resource:

    ItemSplitter - ItemSplitter allow you to easily split your items from your main inventory.

    ItemSplitter is a simple plugin that will help you to split your item easily to two equal stack.
    The original stack will still be in your inventory and the second will be drop at your feet.

    Permission:
    itemsplitter.use

    Command:
    /split <Partial Item Name>

    Keep in mind that it will split every item that contain the word your are tiping.

    Exemple if you have 10 Wooden Arrow and 50000 Wood, it will drop 5 Wooden Arrow and 25000 Wood at your feet.

  7. Steenamaroo

    Hi.
    No, this plug doesn't do those things.
    The npc's don't count as players, in terms of population, and they won't mine or gather or anything like that.

    All you can really do is set them to chase/shoot at anyone in a certain radius, or anyone who shows hostility towards them.
    You can also make them patrol pre-programmed routes with waypoints plugin.
     
    Steenamaroo, Oct 15, 2016

    JimLess submitted a new resource:

    RemoveStructure - Chat command to remove structures

    Permissions:
    removestructure.remove

    Using Permissions:
    As an example, to grant the permission to a user:
    /grant user Player removestructure.remove

    Commands:
    /rm - removes the structure you're looking at. Max distance 100m.

    Notes:
    Dynamic structures are not removed. Also, the doors are not removed. This will be fixed in future versions.

    Be careful, you can't undo your actions.
    Before cleaning, make a backup save.

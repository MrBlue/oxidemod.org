  1. Absolut

    Absolut submitted a new resource:

    NoRespawnCooldowns - With Permissions you will never have Respawn Cooldowns

    Requires the player has NoRespawnCooldowns.allow

    It resets all bed and sleeping bag Cooldowns to '0' on each death.. so you never have cooldowns.

  2. CaptainNebulous

    The iteminstance pool message is nothing to worry about. It is just telling you that there is a lot of objects on your server it has to load. It then automatically adjusts to compensate.

    Not sure about the other two, but try increasing your loadbalancerframebudget in increments of 25 (default is 100) and see if it helps. Do not increase this rapidly, at a certain point (depending on the quality of your server) it will start to cause frame drops.

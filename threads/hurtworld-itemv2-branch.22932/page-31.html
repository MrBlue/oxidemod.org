  11. CaptainNebulous

    Default max ping is 300, not 200. If there is a default setting, that will take over without a command. But to disable ping limitations completely, simply put in "maxping -1"

    However this is not recommended in V1, as high ping has severe effects in V1 starting at about 225 and getting really bad at around 325. In V2 it should be fine to have no ping limit
     
    CaptainNebulous, Jun 2, 2018

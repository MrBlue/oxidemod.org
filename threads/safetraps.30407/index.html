    Safe Traps - Traps won't be triggered by the person placing them

    This affects Bear Traps and Landmines.

    By default, it will prevent players being hurt by their own traps for 60 seconds after placing them or re-arming them.

    To change the time period players are protected from their own traps, go to SafeTraps.cfg and change SafeTime. Change it to 0 for players to always be protected.

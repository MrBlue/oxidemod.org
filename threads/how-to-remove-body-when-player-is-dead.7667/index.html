    OnEntityDeath triggers when the player has already died, however to remove a corpse you would want to use the hook OnEntitySpawned as the body of the player is actually removed when he dies and a corpse is spawned in place.
    Documentation said:
    OnEntitySpawned(BaseNetworkable entity)
    • Called from BaseNetworkable.Spawn
    • No return behavior
    • Called after any networked entity has spawned (including trees)
    Click to expand...
    And to remove it you would just call a KillMessage() on the entity (entity.KillMessage()

  2. DevinXXL

    XP and levels are tied together. That means, if XP go up faster, level does aswell. At least from what I've found out.

    You can easily test that by using a plugin to give 5XP to a player. Their level will rise accordingly, depending on what level they're on.

    That means, if your plugin affects XP gain speed, it should be exactly what you're looking for in terms of level ups

    ExplodingBarrels - Barrels containing rockets can explode

    ExplodingBarrels is a plugin which causes barrels which contain rockets to have a chance to explode on hit, damaging or even killing nearby entities.

    When a player hits a barrel containing a rocket, it will have a chance to explode - on the chance the explode is triggered, the landmine click sound will be played, and the player has some time to get away from the barrel, after which the barrel will explode and deal explosive damage to any players/entities within its...

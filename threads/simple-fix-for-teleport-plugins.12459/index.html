Simple fix for teleport plugins

Discussion in 'Rust Discussion' started by Jey., Oct 27, 2015.

  1. Jey.

    Hey guys

    There's so many teleport mods out there, and to be honest I love them and I hate them.
    I love them because I can find my friends easily. I hate them because it break the game in several ways, the two most obvious are teleporting to and from raids, and teleporting to escape combat.

    There is a really simple solutions to fix this, it can be done in one of two ways:

    1st: When the player teleports, before just before they are moved, drop their entire inventory at their feet. (you will need to make sure they cant pick it up before they TP.
    2nd: If that's not possible, kill the player then teleport them.

    This method will ensure that teleporting is not used to avoid dangerous situations. Players will now return to going out on foot and putting their lives in danger to raid, farm or hunt... The intended game-play of rust will be back to how it should be.

    Now I could build this plugin myself, but that would just be a waste of time a there are already perfectly good TP plugins out there. PLus I'm sure you guys will do a better job of it than me. If you guys could just add in this one extra function the game would be way more fun again.

    Thanks for taking the time to read this, I hope you see why this is important.

    Cheers,

    Jey

    CarCommanderLite - Car spawner with added features

    A minimised version of my CarCommander plugin

    Features:
    Automated car spawns via spawn file
    Auto-spawn limits
    Increased bullet damage to cars (about 1hp per bullet as opposed to the default of 0.005hp)
    Explosion on death
    Vehicle health UI
    Water level detection to prevent players from dumping cars under water
    Disable or restrict player held items whilst driving a car
    Permissions to drive spawned cars

    Permissions

  20. VinylFresh

    For people having trouble with spawns - install spawns database plugin and give yourself permission to use it if needed - in game type /spawns new - now go and stand where you would like a car the spawn on your map - once there type /spawns add - you have now created a spawn for your car at that point now go make more around the map as needed and once finished type /spawns save cars - next go to your config and change max number of cars to spawn at one time to match the amount of spawn points you created for your cars and then change this line "Spawnfile name": "",' - to - "Spawnfile name": "cars", - and finally set the time delay for cars to spawn ( set this to like 1 minute for quick results) now save the config and reload plugin wait 1 min and see if your cars spawn - 1 warning the cars are really really buggy and will desync the shit out of you and maybe even kill you
     
    VinylFresh, Nov 10, 2017

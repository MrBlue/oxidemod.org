    Prison - The place to put those thiefs and rulebreakers!

    Welcome to the prison. We hope your stay with us is as pleasant as yours is for us.

    Prison will allow you to create multiple cells where you can put those rule breakers.
    It will automatically put new prisoners in a cell with the least amount of prisoners in it unless you specify where you want to place them.
    You can specify for how long they are in prison (seconds, minutes, hours or even days) but by default, any sentence time given is in minutes.

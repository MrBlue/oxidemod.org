  14. Slut

    You cannot connected without using localhost/computer ip. This is because most routers do not come with that support. In some cases you can enable it on your router however it is difficult to do. Therefore you must stick to localhost OR your computers IP for everyone who is connecting in your network. For everyone outside your network tell them to use your public IP.

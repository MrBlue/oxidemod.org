  3. cowtrix

    You can enable the UseExclusionDistance boolean on GroundDrillDynamicServer.DrillMachine, and then set the Exclusion Distance to whatever you want. This doesn't prevent players from placing more than 1 drill per zone, but it does mean that other drills within a drill's exclusion distance will decrease it's drop rate. This means that the optimal layout of drills will only be 1 per exclusion distance squared.

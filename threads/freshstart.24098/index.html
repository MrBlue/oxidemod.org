    FreshStart - Removes all entities when killed by another player

    FreshStart removes all entities (excluding prefabs in the exclusion list) when a player dies by another player. It also can remove corpses.

    See: How To: Report Bugs | Oxide

    Features:
    • Removes all entities on death (by another player)
    • Removes corpses
    • Exclusion permission that excludes the player from the removal of entities on death
    • Exclusion list for prefabs

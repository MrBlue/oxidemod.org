  4. Noviets

    Noviets updated No Fall Damage with a new update entry:

    2.0.0

    Fixed:
    Plugin not changing fall damage when respawning or spawning correctly.

    Added:
    Ability to toggle NoFallDmg on and off for all players by using:
    /nfd [enabled|on] or /nfd [disabled|off]

    You can also use "/nfd" to turn fall damage off just for yourself. This is handy if you are using scripts like Fly which enable fall damage again once you stop flying. Both commands require Admin or the nofalldmg.admin permission.

    New Permission Added:
    nofalldmg.admin

  6. Wulf Community Admin

    Draftmission said: ↑
    I can't connect and play on my server. I only get "Connection Attempt Failed". Which files/logs do I upload so that someone can help me solve this?
    Click to expand...
    If you are hosting from home, make sure you open the appropriate ports with your router. If you are connecting locally, use your LAN IP, localhost, or 127.0.0.1 for yourself. Also make sure you set the IP in your startup script to 0.0.0.0, not your router's IP.
     
    Wulf, Dec 26, 2015

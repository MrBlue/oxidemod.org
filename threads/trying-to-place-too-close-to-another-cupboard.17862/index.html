  2. Death Maker

    You should be able to make it work by setting the config line of the influence on cupboards to false. This will allow for Alpha to Omega Cupboards to be set, as well as requiring raiders to have access to 50% of the total cupboards before they can /remove (also alpha is required with that count.) Another thing you may want to consider is changing the radius from 25 to a lower number as at 25 the cupboard radius will allow players to build a base 10x10 base, which for some servers may be huge (100 foundations for 1 cupboard is a bit of overkill.

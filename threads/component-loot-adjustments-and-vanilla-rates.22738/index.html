  2. Dream 5

    Hey Trippy,

    I'm not sure where you can find the default values for loot, but what I would do is just feel it out yourself through experience and feedback.

    What you could do to control this better is this:
    • spawn.min_rate # (default 0.5)
    • spawn.max_rate # (default 1)
    • spawn.min_density # (default 0.5)
    • spawn.max_density # (default 1)
    Decrease these values to reduce density/spawns. It'll be based on the map size you're using for your server. Just feel it out.

    Read the FAQ on Better Loot to help you control what kind of boxes and barrels spawn in your world.
    Revise your loot table until it actually feels balanced to you when you're collecting items.

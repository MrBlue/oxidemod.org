  2. Wulf Community Admin

    Generally Rust will force a wipe every month on the first Thursday. You can manually wipe the world/map by deleting your .sav files, the blueprints by deleting your UserPersistence.db file, and the signs by deleting the Storage.db file.

    I'm not sure about -autoupdate, but I wouldn't rely on it. Keep in mind that if you do use any updater script or setting, it will remove Oxide if you use it as it is a mod to the server.

    Cheeze submitted a new resource:

    Trap Floors - Allow players to turn a floor block into a collapsing trap

    Players can use a command to convert a floor block into a collapsible trap.
    Players who can build in the area of the trap floor, in other words those with cupboard access, can walk over a trap without setting it off. Only unauthorised persons in your building will set off the trap.

    Approx 1/3rd of a second before collapse there is a latch sound... this gives players a chance to react and jump to safety... but they need to be quick.

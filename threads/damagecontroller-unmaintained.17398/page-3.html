I am also a software engineer, and presented with that fact (that nobody except me uses JavaScript), we would also remove support for such a feature.

It was my favorite hobby, and Rust PVE/PVP balance is something I took seriously because it's lacking in the game pretty bad at the moment. Also, I was about 10000 lines into an entire game mode when I found out JavaScript would not be supported, so big kick to the face on that. That was enough to walk away. I appreciate the support from everyone while my MODS were working.

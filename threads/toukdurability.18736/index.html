  1. slowmolife

    slowmolife submitted a new resource:

    ToukDurability - Allow you to configure the durability loss of items.

    With this plug-in you can change the durability loss of your items.

    Set it to 0 to disable durability on your server.

    1.0 is the default value.

    If you put a 0.5 durability goes down twice less than usual.

    feel free to send me suggestions if you want new functionality or anything else!

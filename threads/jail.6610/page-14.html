  2. bitterbals

    jail works fine, but dident tested whit more than 1 player in jail.

    And for al the people who got problems whit the setup. a basic guide.

    First instal 3 plugins on your server,

    1 -Zones Manager for Rust | Oxide
    2 -Spawns Database for Rust | Oxide
    3 -Jail for Rust | Oxide
    4 -Kits option.

    restart server.

    go to your console and type the permis. ex oxide.grant user Bitterbals jail.admin

    Make a building or jail whit ex. 4 cells in the middle.
    open your chat ingame and do

    1- /spawns_new
    2- than you go stand in cell nr1 and do /spawns_add
    3- than you go stand in cell nr2 and do /spawns_add
    4- than you go stand in cell nr3 and do /spawns_add
    5- than you go stand in cell nr4 and do /spawns_add
    6- /spawns_save FILENAME (--- your choice of name ...
    6- ex /spawns_save spawnjail

    now go stand in the middle of your jail and do
    /jail zone 15

    next step is to check your zone id
    /zone "in the middle of the jail"
    and check your id its gonne be nummers or like this zoneid jail_0

    final step enter this,
    /jail add <JailName> <ZoneID> <Spawnfile>'

    ex. /jail add newjail Jail_0 spawnjail

    - You can now send players to jail using '/jail send <playername> <optional:Time> <optional;PrisonName>'.

    ex /jail send bitterbal 3600 newjail ---) sent me for 1hour to jail and i can free myself whit /jail

    Grtzz. and thx for this plugin K1lly0u ^^

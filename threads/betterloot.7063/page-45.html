    I assume you've enabled "enforceBlacklist" to TRUE. This means, it will NOT spawn those items regardless of the content of the LootTable AND will remove all those from aNY containers.

    The Blacklist with "enforceBlacklist" FALSE will prevent only the spawn of the content of the blacklist, but NOT remove those items from any container.
    You could leave the Blacklist empty and instead delete those items from the LootTable.json, then those items/blueprints will never spawn.

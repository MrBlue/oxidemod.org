  1. Nogrod

    dcode submitted a new resource:

    TwigsDecay - Slowly but steadily decays buildings made of twigs.

    TwigsDecay steadily decays buildings made of twigs in an attempt to reduce the overall entity count on servers.

    Configuration (edit config/TwigsDecay.json):
    • damage - The amount of damage a twigs building takes once per timespan (default: 1)
    • timespan - Timespan in minutes for decay updates (default: 15)
    With the default configuration, twig buildings will fully decay in a little more than 6 hours after being built.

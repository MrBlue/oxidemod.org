    Hello Firest,

    Go to your FTB, to delete the map go to /ruste/server/YourFileID/save then delete the "Procedural Map_4000..."
    Where I have said /server/ you will have 1 file in there or at least I do which is my gameservers.com ID e.g.
    /ruste/server/1212121/

    To delete blueprints go to /ruste/server/YourFileID/persistence/players delete all the files inside.

    If you have done as I said you will have wiped your map and blueprints, remember to have your server turned off when doing this..

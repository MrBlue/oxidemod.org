  5. LoneSurvivor

    With Gather manager you can set the total loot amount a node or tree has not just the resources per hit. Rather than typing gather.rate dispenser tree 2, you would just type dispenser.scale ore 2 and you will hit the rock twice as long, which doubles the total loot you can get out of each one.

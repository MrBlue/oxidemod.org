  2. Noviets

    To change Despawn time of vehicles, add VehicleDecayTime to your .bat or autocfg.
    Example: VehicleDecayTime 3600
    Would be 1 hour (in seconds).

    To change to Respawn time of the boxes (CommonLootCrate)
    Download: SpawnConfig for Hurtworld | Oxide
    Search for "CommonLootCrate" and edit it to however you want.
    Once done type: spawn.reload in the console. (If in game press F1 and type: rcon spawn.reload)

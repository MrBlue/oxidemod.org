    LockPickManager - Stops Lockpicking of doors and/or cages

    Stops all Lockpicking of doors and cages, logs location and player that picked the lock. Players can still escape cages and shackles with lockpicks.

    Works with Declaration of war(optional).-only warring guilds can pick locks(enable in config)
    Works with ProtectedZone to allow a lockpicking zone(optional)

    Permission to use commands: lockpickmanager.admin

    Commands(only used with ProtectedZone):
    /addlockpickingflag     //stand in zone or type zoneId after...

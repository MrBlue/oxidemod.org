    Click to expand...
    *Sigh* this question has been asked and answered (I think) three times already.

    Rust Client now prevents Servers from forcing key-bindings on Clients. The plug-in tries to run the command "bind b backpack.open" to bind ("connect") the key to the plug-in's backpack.open command. The client blocks it.

    To fix it (and, again, this has been posted repeatedly above) the user should press [F1] and go to the Console. Type "bind b backpack.open" and press [ENTER]. There will be no reply from the console, but they can now use the key to access the backpack.

    For what it's worth, I also use GameServers.com and the new players who are having issues with it found this fixes the problem.

    Please, for the love of all that is good and holy, try skimming through the last page or two of posts. Just try.

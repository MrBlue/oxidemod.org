  7. Bourtak

    I have found a solution with http://oxidemod.org/plugins/kits.668/

    Create your kit, go in data/Kits_List.json and add " BP" at the end of each Bp you will
    Then, your player can have kits with BP.

    If you will permission, you can use permission of oxide, and give permission to the player then.

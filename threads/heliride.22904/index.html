  1. Colon Blow

    Colon Blow submitted a new resource:

    HeliRide - Players can fly the Patrol Helicopter

    Allows Player to Simulate Flying the patrol helicopter. Player can toggle the Player Helicopter, which will follow player really closely while they are Noclipping. Player can then shoot the helicopters guns, rockets and use spotlight at night.

    Custom cockpit overlay and crosshairs will show up, which can be disabled via config file. Once players helicopters health reaches 30% or lower, they will get a "broken glass" overlay to warn them they are almost dead.

  12. Colon Blow

    Working on improvements for next release. Probably after patch this week.

    - Rockets now fire from the rocket tubes left and right, instead of from underneath.
    - Napalm rockets will all fire from left tube or right tube each round. alternating.
    - Helicopters Pitch is limited. So as it does not allow it to point straight up or down.
    - Helicopter will self level if just sitting still.
    - Player himself no longer receives damage while flying helicopter.
    - Added ability to fly helicopter straight down as if you wanted to land with the Duck button. Helicopter can already go UP with jump button.


    more fixes in the works.

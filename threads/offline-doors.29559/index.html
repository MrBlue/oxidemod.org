    Slydelix submitted a new resource:

    Offline Doors - Automagically closes all doors owned by player when he disconnects

    Offline Doors closes all doors owned by player when he disconnects, useful when player forgets to close them.

    Player can disable this feature using /ofd

    There's no config (yet?)

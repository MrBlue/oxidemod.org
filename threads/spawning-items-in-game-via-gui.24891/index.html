    You do not need a plugin to fly, you can use noclip to fly. You should probably bind it to a key through console: bind p noclip - for example. This would toggle noclip (flying) on and off every time you hit the ''p' key on your keyboard. You do need authentication level 1 or 2 to fly and give yourself items through console.

    As for giving yourself items, you could just use the F1 console, if you do need to give other players item, you should use a plugin called Give. You should also search for specific plugins before asking a question.

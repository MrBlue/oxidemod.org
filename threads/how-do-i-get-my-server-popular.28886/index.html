  4. thepiercedweirdo

    Same as above. Having it listed makes a huge difference. After taking my servers off the listing my population dropped drastically. And be sure to have a detailed but to the point description. Reddit is hit or miss. They have a specific subreddit for listing servers r/playrustservers. It's not high traffic but I've gotten some players off it. Listing it on r/playrust could just get you showered with salt. Having a voter plugin on your server also helps as it increases your position on the server listing faster.

    Having a banner is also important when you list on the servers. Visuals bring clicks. Here's a tutorial on making an animated GIF in GIMP. It may not be pretty the first time around. But it gets the point across. Having a website also helps a little. You generally don't get much traffic to the site. But it raises your profile a little more.

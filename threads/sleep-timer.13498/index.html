    Hello, I come from the server PurePVE a very popular PVE server, and as an Admin I am often in a dilemma of who to clean up and who not to, and I had a Eureka moment since our server uses the "notifier" that amongst other things gives a notification if someone logs on or off...

    so I thought, what if the time of that "log off" got stamped into a log so an Admin like me could check if the player I am looking at, logged out an hour ago or a week ago.

    but on PurePVE we have no decay and no killing, so its up to the Admins to clean out inactives.

    Having some sort of timestamp of last log in/out would be a great tool in getting rid of the right sleepers and not sum1 who just had a crash a couple of hours ago.

  1. Skrallex

    Skrallex submitted a new resource:

    Replenish - Easily reset/replenish loot in storage boxes after a certain amount of time.

    Replenish for Rust allows you to setup large and small storage boxes to regenerate or replenish their loot after a certain amount of time. Loot is only replenished when it is taken from the inventory.

    Features:
    • Very easily setup storage boxes to regenerate loot.
    • Configurable timers for loot replenishing.
    • Permissions included.
    • Replenishing boxes persist through restarts (a file in the Data folder is used to store data).

    1. You don't go to Control Panel to use Notepad++. Just right-click the file, choose "Open with" and click "Look for another app on this PC" or "Other" depending on your OS, and browse to where you installed Notepad++ - then choose the Notepad++ executable.
    2. There's no need to use a program to change the filetype. Open your Folder-settings/File Explorer Options through Control Panel and untick "Hide extensions for known filetypes". Now you can change the filetype by simply changing the name of the file in the folder.

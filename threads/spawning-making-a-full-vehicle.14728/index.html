  5. Noviets

    This is actually easy to do, just use the gameobject of the vehicle when you spawn it (NetInstantiate) to acquire the RestrictedInventory slots. Then populate the slots with new item instances of what you want it to have. Then make sure you do .Invalidate(); else it'll still look like an empty frame.

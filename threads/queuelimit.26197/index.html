    QueueLimit - Limits the amount of people allowed to be in a queue at any one time

    This plugin kicks players from connection queue if the queue is larger than your configured limit. Some servers may not want a queue and some servers may want to limit their queue for network reasons. Regardless I thought it was useful after seeing a suggestion made on the Facepunch Jira.

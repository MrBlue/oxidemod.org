    Most routers will not let you connect to your own external IP address from inside the network. It's not about ports being open or not.

    You can connect using your LAN IP, localhost, or 127.0.0.1 in your F1 console.

    Others outside of your network would connect using your external IP address or from the server list, granted that the ports are forwarded.

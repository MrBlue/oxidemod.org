  2. Dream 5

    In short, the way rust handles skins is that it downloads them as you physically own them through the workshop. As you're using a plugin, whats happening is the content is being downloaded as soon as you craft a skin that you haven't already downloaded. Every player will individually experience this on your server.

    When you craft a skin, just check your steam downloads for workshop content and make sure they're not being stopped from downloading.

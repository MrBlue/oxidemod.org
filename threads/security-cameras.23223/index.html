  15. Steenamaroo

    I was working on this a little while ago for arena-specate.
    I also considered allowing privileged players to have one camera for their base.
    Video Here.

    My intention was to make it that any deployable could serve as a camera. Admin looks at the deployable and types /cctv add,
    then that entity goes into the cctv loop.
    In the video I just used sleeping bags for testing.

    Pressing whatever key cycles through cameras. Pressing numbers jumps to that camera.

    I had two main issues. One was that I was putting the viewer in spectate so space bar allowed him/her to break out and view other players on the server.
    Re-binding space bar didn't work so I guess there's something client side that I can't override?

    The other I've literally forgotten in the space of time it took to write this post.

    I played with the camera world model but didn't really get anywhere. I could 'summon' it, but couldn't work out how to place it properly.

    HideAndSeek - The classic game(mode) of hide and seek, as props

    HideAndSeek is work-in-progress re-make of the Prop Hunt / Hide and Seek game mode for games such as Garry's Mod, and other games. It's currently unfinished, but there's still fun to be had!

    To hide as an item, you'd first need the permission that is documented below. Once you have that, you can use the chat commands or simply use the Left and Right mouse buttons. Enjoy!

    - people are transported to a location that is walled off in a special zone with Invent saved and a Hide and Seek kit added.
    - Game randomly picks X number of people that have hide ability (Maybe takes into account the last people that had hide so they don't get it twice in a row. Could also choose how many hide based on total players that accepted the invite)

    - when people die they get transported back and original invent replaced.

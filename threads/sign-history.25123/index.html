  1. Kisuka

    Kisuka submitted a new resource:

    Sign History - Track changes to signs to record malicious edits.

    This plugin is for moderators or admins to monitor sign changes.

    There had been a few times on our server where someone's sign had been modified to feature vulgar content. Was hard to take the sign owner's word, so I developed this plugin to create a changelog for signs.

    • Simply add signhistory.admin permission to the admin/mod.
    • Approach a sign / canvas, and while looking at it do /history in the chat.

  1. ninco90

    ninco90 submitted a new resource:

    TurretInfo - Check the authorized players in a AutoTurret and the victims of it.

    TurretInfo allows to check the authorized players in each turret. It also keeps a record of the deaths made by each turret. Only the owner of the turret (who places it) and any user with turretinfo.admin permission can check the authorized players, the victims of the turret and clean the list.

    In addition TurretInfo offers the possibility to show in the Global Chat the name of the owner of the turret when it kills a player. As well as motivate in the Global Chat the name of...

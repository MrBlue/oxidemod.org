  6. Aggermor

    There is a Copy-Paste plugin that allows you to copy a building you are looking at and then paste it on the floor. However you can give the command an integer and it will adjust the height relative to the ground coordinate.
    http://oxidemod.org/plugins/copy-paste.716/

    Example:
    /copy deathmatchArena > this will copy the building you are looking at and save it in the oxide/data folder.
    /paste deathmatchArena 100 > this will paste deathmatchArena 100 units above the ground you are looking at.

  2. Kappasaurus

    Once you have Oxide installed simply use Streamline Server's web based file client, or preferably FileZilla (https://filezilla-project.org/). I assume Streamline either gives you access to "server" or "server_identity." You are looking for the "Oxide" folder, once you manage to navigate to Oxide look for "plugins," then you can just drop your plugins in there. Please note: not all plugins on this site are maintained, however the majority are.

  1. Tori1157

    Tori1157 submitted a new resource:

    AngryAuthlevel - Automatically adds users to their respective auth level when they connect.

    View attachment 41048

    I was getting tired of putting in auth levels once i wiped and cleaned the map files. Sure i could copy paste the "users.cfg" file each time, but i'm too lazy for that. So i wrote this plugin, and decided to share it.

    Basically what you have to do is to fill in the respective users STEAMID_64Bit (76561198025298808) into the correct autlevels in the config file. Once the user connects and he does not have his auth level, he'll be given it.

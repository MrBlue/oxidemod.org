    The message is broken up into two entires in the lang file. Not ideal for user expansion.

    Code:
    {% raw %}
      "prtop": "/pr tops",
  "prtop2": "Displays all of your top stats.",

    to

    Code:
    {% raw %}
      "prtop": "",

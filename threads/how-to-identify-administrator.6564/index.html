    in \server\my_server_identity\cfg\

    Create 3 files bans.cfg server.cfg and users.cfg

    In users.cfg add the line
    ownerid yoursteamid "name" "no reason"

    For moderator use
    moderatorid steamid "name" "no reason"

    you need to reboot to take effect.

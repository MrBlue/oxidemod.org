    Usually the first Thursday of each month is a forced wipe (modded or not), and will cause issues if you try restoring an old map. Deleting these two files in server/your_server_identity/ will wipe your server and generate a new map (yours will not have the exact name, but very similar):



    If you want to change the seed, look for or add "server.seed" to your config or startup line. If you want to change your world size, look for or add "server.worldsize"

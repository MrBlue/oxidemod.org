  2. XDarkMario

    also after renaming your save to keep your map across updates do this command in the console to fix floating trees and stuff
    del assets/bundled/prefabs/autospawn/resource

    that will delete all trees and natural spawning stuff and gradually respawn them normally.
    but do keep in mind the more you do this with each update it gets more unstable, i have bypassed like 8-10 updates and now theirs floating debris from structures in the sky and floating recycling systems

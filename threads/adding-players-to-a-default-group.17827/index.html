    Hi, everybody with authlvl 0 (that is normal players) are automaticaly forced into group player. So all you need to do is grant group player permissions you want for them to use, for example deathnotes.see removertool.canremove ect... When a new player comes for the 1st time to the server he will have those permissions cause he will be in group player with this. Also you can create a group called players and give normal players (newcomes) nothing, and after some time you can transfer them to group players with different or more permissions.

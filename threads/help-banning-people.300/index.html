  2. Steven Funke

    Before banning people type "status" in your game console or Web console (For PlayRust.EU customers) after you logged into Rcon with your Rcon password. Then type banid "steamid64" "reason" into your console or Web console and they will be banned. Now you need to kick them with "kick <name>" or simply restart the server. The SteamID64 is the first ID when you type "status". It's a long number which mostly starts with a 7 I think.

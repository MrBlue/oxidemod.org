  4. juk3b0x

    on my server i have a "Animal-Capture Plugin"

    so people can try to capture a sheep for example.

    when they see a sheep, they write /tame sheep
    and then they have 60 seconds to to damage to the sheep.
    for every hit there is a 20% chance that the player "captures the sheep.
    if he succeeds, he gets the item "sheep" in his inventory (which enables him to take the sheep to his castle/town and let it walk around there) and the sheep gets killed

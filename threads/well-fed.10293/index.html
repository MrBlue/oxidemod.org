  16. Colon Blow

    yes, you have to give permission to whichever player or group first.

    if server console you can type:
    oxide.grant group player canbewellfed

    that will give the 'player' group (which is all players by default) max life/hunger/thirst on login and respawn by default.
    you can change whether they get is on login and/or respawn in the config file with true or false for each.

    right now it does not differentiate between the first initial login and any other login. if they are in the group and have access, every login will give them max.
     
    Colon Blow, Nov 21, 2015

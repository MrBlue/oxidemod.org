  2. RustProof

    You need to enable their "GAME" firewall, which is essentially just a specialized UDP firewall. Then punch a hole for 28015 (or whatever you've set your rust port to)

    I've used OVH in the past, including when a lot of community servers were DDoS'd last year, I got an email from OVH stating I was under attack and they were scrubbing my traffic. My server was fine during the attack.

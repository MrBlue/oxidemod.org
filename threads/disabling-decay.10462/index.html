    Currently the decay system in Rust only affects foundations. The rest of the building parts do disappear eventually when the foundations holding them are destroyed by decay (it's a bit more complex than that, since some elements can be held by others.. For instance a corner foundation that decays usually won't take the two walls closing that corner since they are being held by pillars and a ceiling on top. I'm guessing this is tied to stability. Anyways, nothing other than foundations and foundation steps get decay damage as far as I know, except camp fires.

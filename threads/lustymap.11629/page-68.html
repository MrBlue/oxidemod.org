  4. isuelt

    It seems like the overview should be changed since this, "If you have rustio installed you can get your map image from typing <serverip>:<port>/map.jpg into your browser." no longer seems to produce a good map ( for example look at this => http://208.146.44.65:38655/map.jpg )

    Additionally this seems unfair, "Alternativly you can use playrusthq to generate a map for your seed".

    You should recommend people use the map generator on Playrust.io and donate to them for making the Livemap

    To get a good map for your server use a link like this:

    http://playrust.io/map.jpg?level=Procedural Map&size=[mapsize]&seed=[seed number]

    replacing [mapsize] and [seed number] with your server's settings.

    ( i.e. http://playrust.io/map.jpg?level=Procedural%20Map&size=4200&seed=773853231 )

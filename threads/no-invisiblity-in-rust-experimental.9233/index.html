    If you don't mind correcting a few details.

    There is indeed a spectator mode, and you may enable it by typing spectator on the console.
    SHIFT+L is the fly mode.
    SHIFT+P is also a fly mode, but your character you remain where you are, so when you press SHIFT+P again you don't have to fly back where you were.

    Tip: In case someone uses spectator mode, the only way to respawn is to type respawn in the console. (At least that I know of)

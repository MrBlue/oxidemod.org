    Dablin submitted a new resource:

    Blueprint Checker - Check how many blueprints an online player has learnt.

    A simple tool to allow authorised users to check how many blueprints an online player has currently learnt; for the rare situation that knowing such a thing actually becomes important.

    Usage:
    Code:
    {% raw %}
    /bpcheck "username or steamid"
    {% endraw %}
    For example:
    Code:
    {% raw %}
    /bpcheck "Dablin"'Dablin' knows 44 blueprints of 119
    {% endraw %}
    Despite its simplicity, I do have plans to expand on its functionality.

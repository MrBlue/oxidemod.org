  17. Reneb

    No it only checks when the players kills something far away (40m d√©fault)
    It checks his angle of view when he kills and the angle of view 0.3s later (so after the recoil)
    if both are exactly the same => no recoil.

    This check is not perfect, if players move while shooting they might not get detected, so a lot of false negatives
    but i guess it can still help.
    i can't really find another way to do that
